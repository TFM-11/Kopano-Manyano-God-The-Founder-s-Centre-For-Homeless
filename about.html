About Kopano Manyano

Kopano Manyano God The Founder Centre for Homeless, works to restore dignity and support vulnerable people across Pretoria and surrounding areas.

Our Story

The organization started in 2001 at 139 Mears Street, Sunnyside, Pretoria. Founded by Pastor Catherine Mogale, the centre began as a place to help homeless people and victims of abuse, offering counseling, support and basic services.

Leadership

Pastor Catherine Mogale

Director / CEO

With over two decades of dedicated service, Pastor Mogale has been the driving force behind Kopano Manyano's mission to restore dignity and hope to vulnerable communities.
