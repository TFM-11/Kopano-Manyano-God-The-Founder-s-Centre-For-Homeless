Our Mission

To restore human dignity and support vulnerable individuals and communities in their journey from dependency to self-reliance.

Our Vision

To build an enabling environment that develops human capacity and lifts vulnerable families into sustainable livelihoods.

Our Values

Responsibility • Respect • Honesty • Support • Love • Empathy • Competence • Commitment

Programs & Services

We work across homelessness, victim empowerment, HIV/AIDS homecare, drug rehabilitation, youth development and food security.

Homelessness Support

Temporary shelter, family reconnection, skills training and job placement.

Victim Empowerment

Trauma counseling, debriefing and legal awareness programs.

HIV/AIDS & Home Care

Community awareness, home base care, and reintegration support.
